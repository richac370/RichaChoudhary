About Me

Where languages
meet learning design

I grew up between three languages — Hindi, English, and French — and that multilingual lens shapes everything I design. I don't just build courses. I think about how people learn, what keeps them engaged, when they get lost, and how to bring them back.

My background is unusual for an instructional designer: 7 years as a language teacher in India and France, followed by a Master 2 in Pedagogical Engineering at Université de Rennes 2. I'm currently completing an internship at Groupe Lactalis — digitalising Quality training, producing corporate videos, and gamifying internal learning content.

I design with ADDIE and SAM, build in Articulate 360, Vyond, H5P, deploy on Moodle and SharePoint, and integrate AI throughout. The frameworks only work if the pedagogical thinking behind them is solid — and that's where 7 years in the classroom gives me an edge most junior IDs don't have.

I'm looking for a role where I can design complex, ambitious learning experiences — not just tick compliance boxes.
